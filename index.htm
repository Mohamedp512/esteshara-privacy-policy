📋  Summary

Esteshara is a coaching and consultation platform that connects users with professional coaches for 1-on-1 sessions. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over your information. We are committed to handling your data with transparency and in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

 

1. Introduction

Welcome to Esteshara ("we", "us", or "our"). Esteshara is a mobile application available on Android and iOS that connects individuals with professional coaches across specialties including leadership, wellness, career development, finance, and more.

 

This Privacy Policy governs the collection, use, storage, and disclosure of personal information when you use the Esteshara application and any related services. By using Esteshara, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

 

If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of the application.

 

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

 

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

•        Full name and display name

•        Email address

•        Phone number (optional)

•        Profile photo (optional)

•        Session reviews and ratings you submit

•        Messages and comments in the platform

 

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

•        Device type and operating system version

•        App usage data (screens visited, features used)

•        Session timestamps and booking history

•        Firebase Anonymous UID (for guest sessions)

•        Crash reports and diagnostic data

 

2.3 Payment & Transaction Information

•        Booking amounts and session fees

•        Payment method type (Visa / Mastercard — no raw card numbers stored by us)

•        Transaction status and booking confirmations

 

🔒  Note on Payment Security

We do not store raw credit card numbers or payment credentials on our servers. All payment transactions are processed through a certified third-party payment gateway.

 

3. How We Use Your Information

 

Providing Services

To create and manage your account, process bookings, and connect you with coaches.

Session Management

To schedule, confirm, and facilitate video coaching sessions via Google Meet.

Communication

To send booking confirmations, reminders, and important service announcements.

Payment Processing

To process consultation fees, manage coach payouts, and track financial transactions.

Reviews & Ratings

To display approved user reviews on coach profiles to help others make informed decisions.

Safety & Security

To detect fraud, prevent abuse, and protect users and coaches on the platform.

Analytics & Improvement

To understand how the app is used and improve features and performance.

Legal Compliance

To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and government requests.

 

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

 

4.1 With Coaches

When you book a session, your name, booking details, and selected time slot are shared with the coach to facilitate the session.

 

4.2 With Service Providers

We use trusted third-party service providers who process data on our behalf, including:

 

Firebase (Google)

Authentication, Firestore database, push notifications, and crash analytics.

Google Meet

Video conferencing for coaching sessions.

Payment Gateway

Secure processing of consultation fee payments.

Cloud Storage

Profile images and document storage.

 

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority, or where necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Esteshara, our users, or the public.

 

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your personal data may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify users of any such change via email or prominent in-app notice.

 

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specific retention periods:

 

Account Data

Retained for the lifetime of your account plus 30 days after deletion.

Booking Records

Retained for 3 years for financial and audit compliance purposes.

Reviews & Ratings

Retained indefinitely while the coach profile exists.

Payment Records

Retained for 7 years per Saudi financial regulations.

Crash & Diagnostic Logs

Retained for 90 days then automatically deleted.

Anonymous Guest Data

Retained for 30 days after the guest session expires.

 

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email listed in Section 10.

 

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

 

•       End-to-end encryption for data in transit (TLS/HTTPS)

•       Firebase Security Rules restricting access to authorized users only

•       Role-based access control for admin and coach data

•       Firestore atomic transactions to prevent data inconsistency

•       Regular security reviews and vulnerability assessments

 

⚠️  Important

While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to log out of your account when using shared devices.

Right to Access
